We are a small family-run local business, operating in Kirwan since 2012, we deal in all kinds of baked goods but our speciality is traditional Australian baked goods. We started this bakery because we felt there were not many bakeries selling traditional Australian baked goods in the area and we wanted to fix that. We are located conveniently near Willows Shopping Centre. Our baker is an artisan-baker and was trained in Paris. We strive to deliver quality products at the lowest prices. Some of our current customers are James Cook University, the Cowboys and Telstra. We can assure you that you will never leave our store without a smile on your face.
